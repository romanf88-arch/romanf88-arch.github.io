About

As an Online Education Coordinator at CSUMB, I work with faculty to improve online teaching through low-overhead, high-impact practices. My approach emphasizes real teaching (not just course design), student engagement, and clear assessment.

I am particularly interested in how AI can be used thoughtfully in education— supporting equity and efficiency while maintaining academic integrity and meaningful learning.
